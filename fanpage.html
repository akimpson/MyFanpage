Bio

Kim Owens, better known by his stage name Kem, was born July 23, 1969 in Nashville, TN. Kem was raised in Pontiac, MI but there was a time when he lived on the streets of Detroit struggling with a drug addiction.

Kem overcame homelessness and addiction to then fund his first album Kemistry in 2003. Kem won a Billboard Music Award in 2005 for Top Adult R&B Single of the Year, "I Can't Stop Loving You". Three Grammy nominations, 7 Number 1 Hits, and 13 Top Ten Hits later, Kem is an R&B superstar.
